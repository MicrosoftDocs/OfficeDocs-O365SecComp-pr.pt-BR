## <a name="managing-mailboxes-on-delay-hold"></a><span data-ttu-id="62e69-218">Gerenciando caixas de correio em espera de atraso</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="62e69-218">Managing mailboxes on delay hold</span></span>
